Know about Project Hale

'Hale' signifies health and strength therefore the project ventures to provide clean drinking water along with woman empowerment.

Aim of our project


This project aims to distribute portable, inexpensive,efficient water purifiers to rural localities wherein water borne diseases are a common problem.
The aim of the project is to provide every young school going child, adult in household or workplace and old age person with clean drinking water. Requirement as basic as water is a right to every individual irrespective of their socio economic background and therefore this project is a small step towards a large change.
