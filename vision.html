Impact

What is the impact of our project

Economic Impact

The water purifier is cost affective and easily affordable by the underprivileged section of the society. At low cost ,clean drinking water can be provided for over 3 months

Social Impact

To initiate a small step towards a big change this project aims to provide clean drinking water as a basic right as well as necessity to people in rural ans less privileged areas of the country.

Environmental Impact

Plastic used in the structure is food grade plastic which can be recycled so it does not cost on the environment.The used water purifiers can be recycled.
